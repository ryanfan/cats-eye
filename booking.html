Step Two: Check Our Availability

Check our "Events" page to see when the space is available for your event.

Step Three: Contact Us

Email manager@thecatseye.ca with the following information:

  • What group you are writing from
    • Note: Only VUSAC-recognized clubs/associate groups, Victoria College residences, or Victoria College student groups can book the space
  • What day and time you would like the space for (leave yourself time for set-up and clean-up!)
  • What your event will entail
    • Required equipment/facilities (our staff is happy to help set it up!)
    • Whether you will be charging a fee for entry/taking donations
    • Anything else we can help you with
  • Whether this will be an event with alcohol
    • Note: Groups are not allowed to supply their own alcohol; you must use our bar

Step Four: Setting Up the Bar

To have the bar open, you must fill out a risk assessment form, available at: Risk Assessment

After booking your slot with us, contact either the campus or residence life coordinators, who will take you through your risk assessment and ultimately book the bar through Food Services. Only once they have approved your event will the bar be officially open.
  • Bergita Petro (Campus Life Coordinator): bergi.petro@utoronto.ca
  • Shainiya Balachandran (Residence Life Coordinator): shainiya.balachandran@utoronto.ca

Important: Please write to us at least two weeks in advance if you would like to book the bar to allow enough time for Food Services to book a bartender. Leaving your assessment until too late risks you losing the bar!

The bar offers:

  • Beers
  • Wines
  • Mixed Drinks
  • Free snacks

The bar may come with set-up and operational costs - contact us to negotiate this cost.

And That's It!

No forms to fill out, no booking fees! Our staff is happy to help set up and run the event with you, and to answer any questions you may have - just look for the Cat's Eye sweaters!
